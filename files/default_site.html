Welcome to the LMOC Landing Page


LMOC Mission Statement

Live Mission Operation Capability (LMOC) makes United States Air Force warfighter training faster and more accurate. LMOC seeks to use modern technology to make training a more realistic "train as you fight" experience while providing value through increasing readiness and integrating technology. Put simply, LMOC provides better training.

Faster.

LMOC's Goal

The LMOC mission is to increase the value of warfighter training to make the experience accurate, efficient, and satisfying, by streamlining the interconnection of devices and data using Agile to deliver an effective software-based solution.








WarRoom

WarRoom provides a much smaller hardware footprint versus legacy systems. The hyper-converged hardware interfaces easily to VLAN, VPN, and routed networks only requiring high and low side network hardware to interface with the VM network fabric. This allows devices such as LVTs, RRUs, or threats to interface with their new VM-based processing systems.

Better training. Faster.
